Creating a Successful Budget

A budget is a financial plan that helps you track income and expenses, ensuring you live within your means and save for the future.

The 50/30/20 Budget Rule

  • 50% Needs: Essential expenses like housing, utilities, food, transportation
  • 30% Wants: Discretionary spending like entertainment, dining out, hobbies
  • 20% Savings: Emergency fund, retirement, debt payoff, investments

Budgeting Tips

  • Track every expense for at least one month
  • Automate savings and bill payments
  • Build an emergency fund (3-6 months of expenses)
  • Review and adjust your budget monthly
  • Use the envelope method for discretionary spending

Budget Tip

Aim to save at least 20% of your income. If you can't, start with any amount and gradually increase it.
