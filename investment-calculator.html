Understanding Investment Returns

Investment returns vary by asset class. Historically, stocks have returned about 10% annually, bonds 5-6%, and real estate 8-10%. However, past performance doesn't guarantee future results.

Key Investment Concepts

  • ROI (Return on Investment): Percentage gain or loss on your investment
  • Compound Returns: Earning returns on your returns over time
  • Risk vs Return: Higher potential returns usually mean higher risk
  • Diversification: Spreading investments to reduce risk
  • Time Horizon: Longer time periods generally reduce risk

Investment Tips

  • Start investing early to maximize compound growth
  • Diversify across different asset classes
  • Consider low-cost index funds for long-term investing
  • Don't try to time the market
  • Rebalance your portfolio annually
  • Keep investment fees low
