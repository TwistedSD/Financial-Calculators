Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your subject line.

Are the calculator results accurate?

Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and are regularly tested for accuracy. However, results are estimates based on the information you provide. For official figures, please consult with a financial professional.

Do you offer financial advice?

No, we provide educational calculators and information only. We are not licensed financial advisors. For personalized financial advice, please consult with a qualified professional.

Can I suggest a new calculator?

Absolutely! We're always looking to expand our calculator offerings. Use the contact form above with "Feature Request" as the subject to suggest new calculators.
